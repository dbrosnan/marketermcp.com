# Fair-Use Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to all Marketer MCP Starter (free) tier users.

## Intended use

The free tier is designed for small businesses, solo marketers, and creators who use Marketer MCP tools for their own marketing operations.

## Acceptable use

- Running tools for your own business or your clients' campaigns.
- Integrating with your own AI assistants and workflows.
- Testing and evaluating tools before upgrading to a paid tier.

## Prohibited use

- Reselling or proxying access to Marketer MCP tools to third parties.
- Automated bulk usage designed to circumvent tier limits.
- Using tools to generate outputs for commercial redistribution without attribution.
- Attempting to bypass rate limits, quota enforcement, or authentication controls.

## Enforcement

- Accounts exceeding fair-use patterns will receive a warning with 7 days to adjust.
- Persistent violations may result in temporary suspension or migration to a paid tier.
- Appeals can be submitted via email to the address listed on the contact page.

## Monitoring

- Usage is monitored via execution counts, request frequency, and payload patterns.
- No content inspection occurs -- monitoring is limited to metadata and volume metrics.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be communicated via email and site notice at least 14 days before taking effect.
