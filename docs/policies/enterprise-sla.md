# Enterprise SLA and Support Scope

## Availability commitment

- **Monthly uptime target:** 99.9%
- **Measurement:** Uptime is measured as the percentage of minutes in a calendar month during which the MCP hub endpoint returns successful responses to health checks.
- **Exclusions:** Scheduled maintenance (announced 48 hours in advance), force majeure, and issues caused by client-side misuse.

## Service credits

| Monthly uptime | Credit (% of monthly fee) |
|---|---|
| 99.0% -- 99.9% | 10% |
| 95.0% -- 99.0% | 25% |
| Below 95.0% | 50% |

Credits are applied to the next billing cycle. Credits do not exceed 50% of the monthly fee.

## Support response times

| Severity | Description | Response target |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | Service unavailable or data loss risk | 4 hours |
| High | Major feature degraded, no workaround | 8 hours |
| Medium | Feature issue with workaround available | 24 hours |
| Low | Question, enhancement request, minor issue | 48 hours |

Response times are measured during business hours (Mon--Fri, 9am--6pm ET) unless a 24/7 support add-on is contracted.

## Support channels

- Dedicated support email or shared Slack channel (per contract).
- Named account contact for escalation.
- Quarterly business review available on request.

## Security review support

- Enterprise customers can request a security overview document.
- Coordinated vulnerability disclosure is available per `SECURITY.md`.
- Annual penetration test summaries shared on request under NDA.

## Onboarding

- Dedicated onboarding session for initial integration.
- Access to implementation guide and sandbox environment.
- Priority access to new tool beta releases.

## Contract terms

- Minimum term: 12 months (annual) or quarterly with 30-day notice.
- Pricing: volume-negotiated based on expected monthly executions.
- Invoicing: monthly or quarterly per agreement.

## Changes

SLA terms are locked for the duration of the contract period. Changes take effect at renewal.
