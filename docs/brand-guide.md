# Brand and Design Guide (v1)

## Voice attributes

- Confident
- Clear
- Practical

## Voice anti-patterns

- Vague claims without proof
- Overhyped language
- Internal engineering jargon in marketing pages

## Visual direction

- Inspired by Tekko's futuristic electric-blue aesthetic.
- Dark gradient surfaces, rounded cards, high contrast typography.
- Reusable design tokens over page-by-page custom styles.

## Design tokens (starter)

- Colors: `#050610`, `#2b49ff`, `#35ddff`, `#eff2ff`
- Radius scale: `16px`, `24px`, `999px`
- Shadows: soft outer shadow + subtle inner highlight
- Typography: Inter with tight headline tracking

## UI QA gates

- Responsive checks: 390px, 768px, 1280px
- WCAG color contrast checks
- Keyboard navigation and visible focus states
- Reduced motion support for animated effects
