# Paid Media Launch Plan

## Budget assumptions

- Monthly budget: < $10,000 (bootstrap tier)
- Launch window: 60-90 days
- Goal: waitlist signups and qualified beta leads
- Primary conversion event: email submit on waitlist form
- Secondary events: blog engagement, FAQ page visits, time on site > 60s

## Channel allocation (monthly $10k example)

| Channel | % of budget | Monthly $ | Role |
|---------|------------|-----------|------|
| SEM (Google Ads) | 35% | $3,500 | Capture high-intent searchers |
| Paid social (LinkedIn) | 25% | $2,500 | Reach marketing leaders + agency operators |
| Paid social (Meta) | 10% | $1,000 | Retargeting + lookalike audiences |
| Display / video (YouTube) | 15% | $1,500 | Awareness + demo video distribution |
| Testing reserve | 10% | $1,000 | New audience segments, Reddit, X ads |
| Creative production | 5% | $500 | Ad variants, video editing |

## SEM plan

### Ad groups

**Brand:**
- Keywords: `marketer mcp`, `marketermcp`, `budget buddy mcp`
- Landing page: homepage
- Expected CPC: $1-3

**Category — budget tools:**
- Keywords: `marketing budget tracker`, `budget pacing tool`, `media budget management`, `campaign budget alerts`
- Negative keywords: `free template`, `excel`, `google sheets`
- Landing page: /lp/budget-pacing.html
- Expected CPC: $4-8

**Category — marketing ops:**
- Keywords: `marketing ops automation`, `AI marketing operations`, `campaign QA tool`, `attribution QA`
- Negative keywords: `jobs`, `salary`, `course`, `certification`
- Landing page: /lp/marketing-ops.html
- Expected CPC: $5-10

### Negative keyword list (shared)

`free`, `template`, `excel`, `google sheets`, `jobs`, `salary`, `hiring`, `course`, `certification`, `tutorial`, `pdf`, `download`, `reddit`, `review`

## Paid social plan

### LinkedIn

**Audience segments:**
- Segment A (in-house): Marketing Directors/VPs at companies 50-1000 employees, B2B SaaS/tech
- Segment B (agency): Media buyers / Account Directors at marketing agencies, 10-200 employees
- Exclusions: Students, entry-level, non-marketing functions

**Ad formats:**
- Single image ads with Budget Buddy value prop
- Carousel: "5 channel allocation mistakes" (blog-to-ad repurpose)
- Video: 30s Budget Buddy demo clip

**CTA:** "Get early access" linking to /lp/budget-pacing.html or /lp/marketing-ops.html

### Meta (retargeting + lookalike)

- Retarget website visitors (7-day, 30-day windows)
- Lookalike audience from waitlist signups (1% similarity)
- Ad format: single image, link to homepage waitlist

## Display / video plan (YouTube)

- 30s pre-roll: "Budget Buddy in 30 seconds" demo clip
- Targeting: custom intent audiences based on SEM keywords + in-market for marketing software
- Landing page: homepage or /lp/budget-pacing.html

## UTM conventions

All paid media links use this taxonomy:

- `utm_source`: `google`, `linkedin`, `meta`, `youtube`
- `utm_medium`: `cpc`, `paid-social`, `display`, `video`
- `utm_campaign`: `launch-{channel}-{segment}` (e.g., `launch-google-budget`, `launch-linkedin-agency`)
- `utm_content`: ad variant identifier (e.g., `headline-a`, `carousel-allocation`)

## Measurement

| KPI | Target (month 1) | Target (month 3) |
|-----|-------------------|-------------------|
| Waitlist signups | 200 | 800 |
| Cost per signup | < $25 | < $15 |
| Click-through rate (SEM) | > 3% | > 4% |
| Click-through rate (social) | > 0.8% | > 1.2% |
| Landing page conversion rate | > 8% | > 12% |

## Optimization cadence

- Daily: check pacing by channel (use Budget Buddy)
- Weekly: review CPA by ad group/segment, pause underperformers
- Bi-weekly: refresh ad creative to combat fatigue
- Monthly: reallocate budget based on CPA and conversion data
