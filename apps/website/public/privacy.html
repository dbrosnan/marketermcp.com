Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

What we collect

When you use Marketer MCP, we collect only the data necessary to operate the service:

  • Account information: email address and organization name provided during signup.
  • API usage data: request timestamps, tool names called, response codes, and latency metrics. We use this for reliability monitoring and rate limiting.
  • Tool input/output: data you send to MCP tools (e.g., budget figures, channel spend) is processed in memory to generate responses and is not persisted after the request completes.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store your platform credentials (Google Ads, Meta, etc.).
  • We do not use your tool input data to train models or share it with third parties.
  • We do not place tracking cookies on this website. We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track individual users.

How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the Marketer MCP service.
  • To monitor service reliability and enforce rate limits.
  • To communicate product updates and launch information you opted into.

Data retention

Account information is retained while your account is active. API usage logs are retained for 30 days for operational purposes. Tool input/output data is not persisted beyond the request lifecycle.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@marketermcp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We will notify registered users by email before making material changes to this policy. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
