Pricing FAQ

What's included in the free beta?

Full access to Budget Buddy, up to 3 team members, 1,000 API calls per month, community support, and all new beta tools as we release them. No credit card required.

How long does the beta last?

We haven't set an end date. The beta will run until we're ready to launch paid plans. We'll give plenty of notice before any pricing changes.

Will my pricing change when you launch paid plans?

Beta users will keep free access during the beta. When we launch Pro, beta users get 50% off for life as founding members. We'll communicate clearly before any changes.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. You can upgrade to Pro or Enterprise when they launch, or stay on the free tier if it fits your needs. Downgrades will be available between billing cycles.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. When Pro and Enterprise launch, we'll offer annual plans with a discount (typically 2 months free). Beta users will get the same founding-member discount on annual plans.
