About Marketer MCP

Marketing tools should work for marketers.

We build purpose-built AI tools for marketing teams — not retrofitted developer utilities. Our mission is to give every marketing team access to sophisticated operations through tools designed for how marketers actually work.

Our mission

We're democratizing marketing AI through open standards. Marketer MCP is built on the MCP protocol — an open standard for AI tool discovery and execution. That means no vendor lock-in, no black-box APIs, and no adapting developer tooling to fit marketing workflows. We make sophisticated marketing operations accessible to every team, regardless of size.
